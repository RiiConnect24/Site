RiiConnect your Wii.

RiiConnect24 brings back WiiConnect24, pushing content to your Wii.
How to Connect


Services

Here are the services that RiiConnect24 supports.

Forecast Channel

Get local and worldwide weather on your Wii. You can see the weather around the world on a virtual globe.

News Channel

Get national and worldwide news on your Wii. You can see the news around the world on a virtual globe or in a slideshow.

Everybody Votes Channel

Vote on fun polls and see what RiiConnect24 community has voted on.

Nintendo Channel

Send demos to a DS system and watch videos such as the Nintendo Week series. Currently just used for nostalgia.

Wii Mail

Send messages or game data to other people connected to RiiConnect24.

Check Mii Out Channel

Share Miis with the community and enter them in contests. The contests aren't working yet.

Why use RiiConnect24?

RiiConnect24 is a great addition to your Wii, allowing you to use discontinued Channels that were part of WiiConnect24 again. It makes your Wii as lively as it once was and once again lets your Wii have contact with the outer world. Whether this will be your first time using these Channels or want some nostalgia, you will enjoy having RiiConnect24 on your Wii.
If you want to play games online that had their online functionality removed with the discontinuation of Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection, please check out Wiimmfi.
