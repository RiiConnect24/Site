Wii Speak Channel

Voice chat with people on your Wii.

Obtain using Auto-WiiWare-Patcher


The Wii Speak Channel can be used again, thanks to combined efforts with RiiConnect24 and Wiimmfi. If you have a Wii Speak, you can register Wii Friends with RiiConnect24 and chat over Wiimmfi. Clicking the blue button above will take you to the download page of auto-wiiware-patcher. The Windows version of the tool has an option to download and patch the Wii Speak Channel. If you don't have a Wii Speak, Amazon sells them for a low price.
