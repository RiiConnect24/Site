News Channel

Read news on your Wii.


The News Channel works with RiiConnect24, and you can read news in 7 different languages depending on what language your Wii is set to. They all have the location where the article takes place in on the Globe (location data comes from Google Maps API) and has pictures associated with the article. Plus, you can view the headlines on the Wii Menu. See below to see what news sources are used for each language.

We have tried to pick the best news sources for each language, and especially looked for sources that included a location in the article. That's because we want the article to appear on the Globe! We've also searched for sources that send out enough news.


  • List of News Sources

  • Japanese
    Reuters
  • English (America)
    Associated Press
  • English (Canada)
    The Canadian Press (through Toronto Star) for national news, Associated Press for other news
  • English (Europe)
    Reuters
  • German
    AFP and SID for sports news (through Spin.de)
  • French
    AFP (through Le Point)
  • Spanish (America)
    Associated Press
  • Spanish (Europe)
    AFP (through rfi.fr)
  • Italian
    ANSA
  • Dutch
    ANP (through Nieuws.nl)
