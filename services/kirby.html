Kirby TV Channel

This service has been brought back thanks to Striim Network.


The Kirby TV Channel was a free downloadable Wii Channel application that showed episodes of Kirby: Right Back at Ya!. It was made available on June 23, 2011 and added new episodes until December 1, 2011. The channel was revived for a second run on April 5, 2012[1] and lasted until September 13, 2012. The application required a Wi-Fi connection to use. 51 episodes were available during the first run; the second run included the first 48. Episodes were ordered according to the original Japanese broadcast. Episode 38, A Novel Approach, was never made available.
